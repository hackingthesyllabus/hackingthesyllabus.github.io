Services

Structured, research-informed workshops designed to be engaging and serious at the same time. Sessions are adaptable in tone, from strategic planning to team cohesion and equity dialogue.

Strategic Vision and Future Alignment

Clarify direction, surface tradeoffs, and align leadership around priorities and execution.

Leadership and Cross-Level Alignment

Integrate perspectives across hierarchy to reduce silos and strengthen organizational coordination.

Culture, Trust, and Team Cohesion

Strengthen norms, accountability, and psychological safety to improve collaboration.

Equity and Inclusion Dialogue

Facilitate constructive conversations that deepen perspective integration and strengthen organizational durability.

Formats

Two-hour workshops, half-day intensives, full-day sessions, and multi-day engagements for groups of four to twenty. Ideal group size is eight to twelve.

Pricing

Engagements typically range from 1,500 to 15,000 CAD depending on duration, preparation, stakeholder groups, and documentation needs.

About

Ikee Gibson is a PhD candidate whose facilitation approach is grounded in empirical research and practical experience. The goal is to help organizations think together with clarity and momentum.
