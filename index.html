Note on method. Workshops use a LEGO based facilitation approach inspired by serious play style methods. This is not a claim of LEGO Serious Play certification.

Process

Simple structure. Strong preparation. Clear outcomes. You get a session that feels engaging, and a result that leadership can use.

Step 1

Discovery and outcome

We clarify the outcome, participants, constraints, and what success looks like.

Step 2

Design and prework

I design the session flow and light prework so the room starts with the right context.

Step 3

Facilitation and synthesis

We run the workshop. You leave with themes, decisions, and next steps that are easy to carry forward.

Formats

Two hour workshop. Three hour workshop. Half day. Full day. Three day engagement with multiple stakeholder groups. Group sizes can be four to five up to twenty. Ideal size is eight to twelve.
