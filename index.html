What I help with

Strategic visioning and alignment

Get people out of slide mode and into sensemaking. Build shared understanding of priorities, tradeoffs, and next steps.

  • Vision and direction setting
  • Operating model clarity
  • Cross team alignment

Culture, trust, and tough conversations

Create a disarming structure to talk about what is usually left unsaid. Useful after growth, reorgs, or demographic change.

  • Psychological safety and norms
  • Conflict and misalignment
  • Equity diversity and inclusion sensemaking

Stakeholder integration

Bring different levels of the organization into the same room to map the system and reduce silo thinking.

  • Leadership and frontline integration
  • Shared system map
  • Commitments across roles

Research quality outputs

Capture the insights with clear documentation that leadership, HR, and teams can act on.

  • Workshop summary and themes
  • Recommendations and risks
  • Optional full report

How it works

  1. Short discovery call to define the outcome and participants
  2. Light prework to clarify context and constraints
  3. Facilitated build and share activities that surface assumptions
  4. Synthesis and decision making
  5. Written output and next steps
